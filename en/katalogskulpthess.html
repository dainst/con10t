In the Department of Archaeology of the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki a research group of PhD candidates and graduate students was formed in 1991 under the supervision of Professors G. Despinis, Th. Stefanidou- Tiveriou and Emm. Boutiras. The group has been working since then on the research project of development of the photographic archive of sculptures kept in the Archaeological Museum of Thessaloniki. The Archaeological Museum of Thessaloniki keeps sculptures dating from the archaic to the late imperial period and found mainly in the area from various regions of Macedonia and Thrace. This archive is used for the publication of the sculptures of the Museum. Until today only the first volume has been published: G. Despinis, Th. Stefanidou- Tiveriou, Emm. Boutiras, List of sculptures of the Archaeological Museum of Thessaloniki (1997). The second volume in which a bigger number of writers participate is in press, while the material that will constitute the third volume of series has already been collected. The archive holds currently over 3.200 photographs.

Arachne is used to mirror that archive and to update it with further volumes.
