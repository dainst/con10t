The photo archive

The photo archive of the Madrid department of the DAI contains images from the department's projects, among these Zambujal, Centccelles, Ampurdan, Fuente Alamo and Sizandro-Alcabichel. Photos of important peoole and the institute's history are also part of the archive.
The digital content can also be filtered by location or the material of the object.
Illuminations in Spanish codices that were digitized as a part of the project Emagines can also be found in the photo archive.
Further subcategories can be chosen through the faceted search on the left hand side of Arachne.
