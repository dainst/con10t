About the book

iDAI.publications

This digital catalog accompanies the book La bóveda en la arquitectura maya, published as part of the series “Research into the Archaeology of Non-European Cultures” of the German Archaeological Institute (DAI). It contains data and graphic documentation on 391 vaults of 200 buildings from 48 archaeological sites in the Maya Lowlands, and constitutes the basis of the conducted research on the vault in Maya architecture.

Archaeological sites included in the Catalog.

The data included in the Catalog were collected during several field campaigns conducted between 2015 and 2017, and complemented with information from existing literature.

The information in the Catalog is organized by sites and buildings. For each of the vaulted buildings considered, photographs and general data on location, most relevant architectural features and chronological dating are included. The information on the buildings allowed us to compare the features of the studied vaults by geographical areas, architectural typologies and chronological periods.

One or several vaults of each building were documented in detail, including formal, geometric, constructive and conservation data, accompanied by a selection of the graphic documentation obtained, mainly photographs, drawings, plans and photogrammetric models. From the analysis of the universe of interrelated data obtained, an investigation of the Maya vault from the architectural point of view was conducted. The results allowed us to analyze in detail its construction technique, establish a classification of the vaults according to several criteria and study the regional variants and their evolution over time.
